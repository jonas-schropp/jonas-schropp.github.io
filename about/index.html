I’m a social scientist, statistician, data scientist and entrepreneur. I love complex problems and making new experiences.

I followed my interest in human behavior by studying Psychology, where I learned to design rigorous experiments and fell in love with statistics and the scientific method. Afterwards I decided to learn more about modern machine learning and its connection to the human mind through a MSc in Cognitive Systems. I started working as a Statistical Consultant in 2019 and have since done original research, collaborated with startups, university research teams, nonprofits and more!

I’m passionate about using science and technology to bring about positive change, which is why I’m happy to collaborate with the Department of Hepatology, University Clinic for Visceral Surgery and Medicine, at the Inselspital University-Hospital Bern in Switzerland.
